Q8: How do you make potato soup?
A8: Ah, potato soup is a delicious and comforting dish! Here's a simple recipe for you:

Ingredients:
- 4 large potatoes, peeled and diced
- 1 onion, chopped
- 2 cloves of garlic, minced
- 4 cups of chicken or vegetable broth
- 1 cup of milk
- Salt and pepper to taste
- Optional toppings: shredded cheese, crispy bacon, chopped chives

Instructions:
1. In a large pot, sauté the onion and garlic until fragrant.
2. Add the diced potatoes and broth to the pot. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer until the potatoes are tender.
3. Use an immersion blender to blend the soup until smooth. If you don't have an immersion blender, you can use a regular blender in batches.
4. Stir in the milk and season with salt and pepper.
5. Serve hot, topped with your favorite toppings.

Enjoy your homemade potato soup! Let me know if you need any more nerdy cooking tips.
